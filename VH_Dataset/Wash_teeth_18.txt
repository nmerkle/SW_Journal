Wash teeth
Walk into to bathroom, grab toothpaste and a tooth brush, clean teeth. Then return all item after rinsing teeth.


[Walk] <bathroom> (1)
[Walk] <tooth_paste> (1)
[Find] <tooth_paste> (1)
[Grab] <tooth_paste> (1)
[Find] <toothbrush> (1)
[Grab] <toothbrush> (1)
[Find] <teeth> (1)
[Wash] <teeth> (1)
[Rinse] <teeth> (1)
[Rinse] <toothbrush> (1)
[Find] <toothbrush_holder> (1)
[PutBack] <toothbrush> (1) <toothbrush_holder> (1)
[PutObjBack] <tooth_paste> (1)
